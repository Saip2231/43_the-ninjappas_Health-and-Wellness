# 43_the-ninjappas_Health-and-Wellness

Our problem comes under the domain Health and fitness
with the awareness in the field of health and fitness my team has decided to help the people aiming to get fit and understand the nutritional requirements for it. we are going to approach this problem by aiming to develop a user-friendly platform that simplifies fitness planning. This platform will give personalised routines based on their goals and physical condition with valuable suggestions and give a vast variety of exercises where the user can choose what to do.
